ECAMPUS Buzz Ś Master Product Requirements Document (PRD)
1. Product Overview
Product Name
ECAMPUS Buzz
Product Vision
ECAMPUS Buzz is a mobile-first campus social ecosystem where students can discover clubs, participate in discussions, vote in polls, attend events, and collaborate inside structured micro-communities.
The platform combines:
* Club ecosystems
* Social feeds
* Poll systems
* Event systems
* Community moderation
* Student engagement tools
The product direction is:
* futuristic
* immersive
* premium
* motion-heavy
* highly interactive
* visually clean
* modern dark UI
The final UI should feel closer to:
* premium startup websites
* modern 3D portfolio experiences
* interactive community platforms
NOT like:
* traditional university portals
* plain dashboards
* generic bootstrap websites
